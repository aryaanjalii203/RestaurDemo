Our philosophy

A menu that
changes with the season.

We cook close to the source. Every morning begins at the market, and the menu is written from whatever arrives — vegetables picked that week, fish landed that day, herbs from the rooftop above the dining room.

The room seats just forty. That's deliberate. It lets us treat every plate, and every guest, with the attention a great meal deserves.

The kitchen

Chef Elena Marchetti

Trained across Florence, Lyon, and Copenhagen, Elena opened Maison Noir to cook the way she eats at home — generous, seasonal, and unfussy, with technique hiding quietly underneath.

Her tasting menu has earned a citywide following and a waitlist that runs weeks deep.
